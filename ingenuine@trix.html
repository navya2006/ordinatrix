INGENUINE@TRIX



Vulnerability is the birthplace of innovation creation and change

  1. 1. This event is open for students of classes VI to XII.
  2. 2. Only 1 team with 2 students can participate from each school.
  3. 3. Only first 20 registrations will be entertained for this event.
  4. 4. Students must come with an idea /solution to one of the existing/future problems of mankind and/or the world we live in.
  5. 5. They will then present it using the creative suite of ‘ADOBE SPARK’
  6. 6. The teams will be oriented on the softwares, though pre hand knowledge will be helpful.
  7. 7. Each team will get 2 systems to work upon.

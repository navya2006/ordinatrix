SNAP@TRIX



Capture time in space!

  1. 1. This event is open for students of classes VI-XII.
  2. 2. Only 1 team with 2 students can participate from each school.
  3. 3. The topic will be declared on the spot.
  4. 4. Participants are required to do as directed in the given time frame.
  5. 5. Photos should be clicked within the given area,anyone trespassing will be disqualified.
  6. 6. The pictures must be submitted in a pen drive with minimal editing allowed on LIGHTROOM and PHOTOSHOP.
  7. 7. Participants should get their own cameras,tripod,transfer cable, card readers,laptops,pen drives etc. The host school will not provide any equipment.
